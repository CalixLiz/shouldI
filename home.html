Why should I travel to Japan in 2020 >> Tokyo 2020, the mystery of the east, amazinly healthy food, anime, studios Ghibli museum... all the talk I've heard from my friends about how fantastic Japan is but is it really? Let's find out.

アート

Art

Japanese art covers a wide range including ancient pottery, ink painting and calligraphy, ukiyo-e paintings and woodblock prints, ceramics, origami, and manga.

フード

Food

Japanese cuisine (和食 washoku) have developed through centuries is based on rice and miso soup, there is an emphasis on seasonal ingredients. Side dishes often consist of fish, pickled vegetables, and vegetables cooked in broth. Raw fish is common as sashimi or sushi.

設計

Design

Zen style focuses on eliminating any unnecessary frills or decors.  These arts are often described as aesthetics of subtraction, because they let boundless beauty and abundance emerge from less, rather than from more.

ファッション

Fashion

There are many styles of street fashion in Japan, created from a mix of both local and foreign labels. Some of these styles are extreme and avant-garde.
